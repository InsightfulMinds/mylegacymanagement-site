Sean's Story

Why legacy?

Sean Vargas grew up watching families around him navigate some of the hardest moments of their lives — a parent's illness, a spouse's sudden passing, an estate left in probate for years. What struck him wasn't just the grief. It was the confusion. The lack of preparation. The sense that no one had ever sat down with these families and explained what they had, what they were missing, and what they could do about it.

That gap became his mission. After becoming a licensed insurance agent in California, Sean built My Legacy Management from the ground up as an independent agency — not tied to any single carrier, not driven by quotas, not incentivized to push any particular product. Just an honest partner who shops the whole market on your behalf and explains every option in plain language.

Based in Oceanside, California, Sean has helped hundreds of families across San Diego, Orange, and Riverside Counties find the right Medicare coverage, establish final expense plans, structure life insurance for estate planning, and build retirement income they can't outlive. He works alongside clients' attorneys and CPAs to make sure the insurance layer supports the full picture.

When he's not working with clients, Sean is active in his local community — because legacy isn't just a financial term. It's what you leave behind in the people around you.
