FAQ

Common questions

When should I enroll in Medicare?
Your Initial Enrollment Period starts 3 months before the month you turn 65 and ends 3 months after. Missing this window can result in lifetime late-enrollment penalties. If you're still covered by employer insurance, different rules apply — we walk you through the exact timing during your free consultation.
What's the difference between Medicare Supplement and Medicare Advantage?
Medicare Supplement (Medigap) covers your out-of-pocket costs under Original Medicare — giving you maximum provider flexibility. Medicare Advantage replaces Original Medicare through a private insurer, often with lower premiums but network restrictions. Which fits you depends on your health, budget, and travel habits. We compare both side by side during your review.
Do you charge for consultations?
Never. Consultations are completely free. As an independent agent, we're compensated by the insurance carrier after you enroll — not by you. This also means we have no incentive to push you toward a higher-priced plan.
What types of life insurance do you work with?
Term life, whole life, final expense, and indexed universal life (IUL). Each serves a different purpose — term for pure income protection, whole life for permanent guaranteed coverage, final expense for seniors seeking affordable guaranteed-issue options, and IUL for tax-advantaged growth tied to market indexes with a guaranteed floor against loss.
Can you help with estate planning?
We specialize in the insurance side of estate planning — life insurance structures, trust-owned policies, beneficiary optimization, and coordination with your overall plan. We work alongside your attorney and CPA to ensure everything aligns. We do not provide legal or tax advice directly.
What areas do you serve?
We primarily serve San Diego, Orange, and Riverside Counties — including Oceanside, Escondido, Temecula, Murrieta, Irvine, and surrounding communities. Many products are available statewide across California.
